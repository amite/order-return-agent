# Communication Templates

## Return Approved - Standard Customer

"Great news! Your return has been approved.

I've created RMA #[RMA_NUMBER] for your [ITEM_NAME]. Your prepaid shipping label is ready to download, and you'll receive an email with all the details shortly.

Once we receive your return (typically 3-5 business days after you ship it), we'll process your refund of $[AMOUNT] within 3-5 business days.

Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

## Return Approved - VIP Customer

"Thank you for your patience, [CUSTOMER_NAME]. I'm pleased to confirm your return has been approved.

As a [GOLD/PLATINUM] member, your return will receive priority processing. I've created RMA #[RMA_NUMBER] for your [ITEM_NAME], and your expedited refund of $[AMOUNT] will be processed within 1-2 business days after we receive the item.

Your prepaid return label is being sent to your email now. You'll find it arrives at your inbox within the next few minutes.

We truly appreciate your continued loyalty. Is there anything else I can assist you with?"

## Return Denied - Time Expired

"I've reviewed your order #[ORDER_NUMBER], and I can see it was placed [DAYS] days ago.

Unfortunately, our standard return window for [CATEGORY] items is [WINDOW] days, which means we're outside the timeframe for a full refund.

However, I don't want to leave you without options. I can offer you:
- Store credit for 50% of the purchase price ($[AMOUNT])
- An exchange for a different item within the same price range

Would either of those alternatives work for you?"

## Return Denied - Final Sale

"Thank you for reaching out about order #[ORDER_NUMBER].

I can see that [ITEM_NAME] was marked as a Final Sale item at the time of purchase. Final Sale items are non-returnable and non-refundable, which is indicated during checkout.

I understand this may be disappointing. While I can't process a return, I can [ALTERNATIVE_OPTION - e.g., offer 10% discount on next purchase, help you find a friend/family member who might want it, etc.].

Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

## Return Escalation - Damaged Item

"I'm very sorry to hear that your [ITEM_NAME] arrived damaged. That's absolutely not the experience we want you to have.

Damaged items require special handling, so I'm going to connect you with our Quality Control specialist who can resolve this immediately. They'll help you with a replacement or full refund, whichever you prefer.

I've shared all the details of our conversation with them, so you won't need to repeat yourself. They'll reach out to you within [TIMEFRAME].

Again, I apologize for the inconvenience, and thank you for your patience."

## Return Escalation - Fraud Flag

"Thank you for your patience while I reviewed your account.

To ensure we provide you with the best service, I'd like to connect you with one of our account specialists who can assist you further with this return request. They'll be able to review your specific situation and provide personalized assistance.

You should expect to hear from them within 24 hours. Is there anything else I can help you with in the meantime?"

## Multiple Orders Found

"I found [NUMBER] orders associated with [EMAIL_ADDRESS]. To process your return accurately, could you help me identify which order you're referring to?

Here are your recent orders:
- Order #[NUMBER_1]: [ITEMS] - Placed [DATE]
- Order #[NUMBER_2]: [ITEMS] - Placed [DATE]
- Order #[NUMBER_3]: [ITEMS] - Placed [DATE]

Which order contains the item(s) you'd like to return?"

## Item Not Returnable (But Empathetic)

"I understand you'd like to return [ITEM_NAME] from order #[ORDER_NUMBER].

After reviewing our policies, this particular item falls under our non-returnable category because [REASON]. This restriction is in place due to [EXPLANATION - e.g., hygiene standards, customization, etc.].

I know this isn't the answer you were hoping for. While I can't accept a return, I want to make this right. [OFFER ALTERNATIVE - e.g., store credit, donation option, discount on future purchase].

How would you like to proceed?"

## Refund Status Inquiry

"I'd be happy to check the status of your return!

Looking at RMA #[RMA_NUMBER], I can see that:
- Your package was received at our warehouse on [DATE]
- The item passed inspection on [DATE]
- Your refund of $[AMOUNT] was processed on [DATE]
- The funds should appear in your [PAYMENT_METHOD] within [TIMEFRAME]

If you don't see the funds by [SPECIFIC_DATE], please reach out and we'll investigate further with our finance team.

Is there anything else you'd like to know?"

## Partial Approval (Mixed Eligibility)

"I've reviewed all the items you'd like to return from order #[ORDER_NUMBER]. Here's what I can do:

**Eligible for Return:**
- [ITEM_1]: Approved (within 30-day window)
- [ITEM_2]: Approved (within 30-day window)
- Total refund for these items: $[AMOUNT]

**Not Eligible:**
- [ITEM_3]: Outside 90-day window for electronics
- Alternative: I can offer store credit for $[AMOUNT] (50% value)

I can process the RMA for the eligible items right now. For [ITEM_3], would you like to accept the store credit offer, or would you prefer to keep it?

Let me know how you'd like to proceed!"

## Technical Issue / System Error

"I apologize, but I'm experiencing a technical issue while processing your return. This is on our end, not yours.

I've documented the problem and submitted a ticket to our technical team. Your order number is [ORDER_NUMBER], and your request has been prioritized.

You'll receive an email update within 24 hours with next steps. In the meantime, if you need immediate assistance, you can also reach our support team at [CONTACT_INFO].

I'm sorry for the inconvenience, and thank you for your patience."

## Gift Return - No Receipt

"I can certainly help with returning a gift!

Since you don't have the order information, let me see if I can locate it. Could you provide:
- The email address that might have been used for the order?
- Approximate date the gift was purchased?
- Description of the item(s)?

Once I find the order, I'll be able to process your return as store credit, which you can use for anything else in our store. Does that work for you?"

## Holiday Extension Reminder

"Good news! Since your order was placed during our holiday season (November 15 - December 31), your return window is automatically extended to January 31.

That means you have until [DATE] to return [ITEM_NAME] from order #[ORDER_NUMBER]. You still have [DAYS] days remaining.

Would you like to proceed with the return now, or do you need more time to decide?"

## Customer Expressing Frustration

"I completely understand your frustration, and I sincerely apologize for [SITUATION]. You have every right to be upset.

Let me see what I can do to make this right. [PROPOSE SOLUTION OR ESCALATION]

Your satisfaction is important to us, and I want to ensure we resolve this to your satisfaction. If what I've proposed doesn't work, I can connect you with my manager who may have additional options. What would you prefer?"

## Successful Resolution - Closing

"I'm glad I could help you with your return today! Here's a quick summary:

- RMA #[RMA_NUMBER] created
- Return label sent to [EMAIL]
- Expected refund: $[AMOUNT]
- Timeline: [TIMEFRAME]

You'll receive a confirmation email shortly with all these details. If you have any questions, just reply to that email or reach out to us anytime.

Thank you for shopping with us, and I hope to assist you again in the future. Have a great day!"

## Unable to Locate Order

"I've searched our system using [ORDER_NUMBER/EMAIL], but I'm not finding any matching orders.

Let's try a few things:
1. Could you double-check the order number? Sometimes there's a typo.
2. Is it possible the order was placed under a different email address?
3. Do you have the confirmation email from when you placed the order?

If you can provide any of this information, I'll be happy to search again. Alternatively, I can connect you with our Account Research team who has access to additional lookup tools.

What would you prefer?"
