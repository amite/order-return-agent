# Return Policy

## Standard Return Policy

### General Returns (30 Days)
All standard merchandise can be returned within **30 days** of purchase for a full refund.

**Requirements:**
- Item must be in original condition
- Tags must be attached for clothing
- No signs of wear or damage
- Original packaging preferred but not required
- Receipt or order number required

**Refund Method:**
- Refunds processed to original payment method
- Processing time: 3-5 business days after receipt
- No restocking fee

### Electronics Returns (90 Days)
Electronics and technology products have an extended **90-day** return window.

**Requirements:**
- Must include all original accessories, cables, and manuals
- Original packaging required
- Serial numbers must match
- No physical damage
- 15% restocking fee applies

**Refund Calculation:**
- Refund = Purchase Price - (Purchase Price Ã— 0.15)
- Example: $500 item = $425 refund

### Clothing and Footwear (30 Days)
Clothing, shoes, and accessories can be returned within **30 days**.

**Requirements:**
- Tags must be attached and intact
- Unworn and unwashed
- No stains, odors, or alterations
- Original packaging not required
- Free returns, no restocking fee

### Final Sale Items (Non-Returnable)
The following items are **final sale** and cannot be returned:

- Clearance items marked "Final Sale"
- Personalized or customized products
- Intimate apparel and undergarments
- Digital products and software
- Special edition collectibles (some exceptions for VIP customers)

## VIP Extended Return Policy (120 Days)

### Gold and Platinum Tier Benefits
Customers with Gold or Platinum loyalty status enjoy an extended **120-day** return window.

**Additional Benefits:**
- No restocking fees on electronics
- Expedited refund processing (1-2 business days)
- Priority customer service
- Prepaid return labels included with shipment

### Eligibility:
- Gold Tier: Members with $2,000+ annual purchases
- Platinum Tier: Members with $5,000+ annual purchases

## Return Window Calculation

The return window begins on the **order date**, not the delivery date.

**Examples:**
- Order placed: January 1
- Delivered: January 5
- 30-day window ends: January 31 (not February 4)

## Refund Processing Timeline

1. **Return Initiated:** Customer creates RMA and receives label
2. **Item Shipped:** Customer drops off package
3. **In Transit:** Shipping typically takes 3-7 business days
4. **Received at Warehouse:** Item scanned and inspected
5. **Inspection Complete:** Quality check (1-2 business days)
6. **Refund Issued:** Processed to original payment method
7. **Funds Available:** 3-5 business days for bank processing

**Total Timeline:** Typically 10-14 business days from shipment

## International Returns

For international orders, customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item is defective. Different return windows may apply based on country regulations.

## Holiday Return Extension

During the holiday season (November 15 - December 31), the return window is automatically extended to **January 31** of the following year for all eligible items.
